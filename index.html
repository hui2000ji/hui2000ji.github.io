Huiyu CAI 蔡辉宇¶

I am a fourth-year undergraduate at the Department of Machine Intelligence, Peking University. I was previously a member of the Language Computing and Web Mining Group, led by Prof. Xiaojun Wan. This autumn, I will continue my studies at MILA under the supervision of Jian Tang.

I am interested in deep generative models, graph representation learning and their wide applications, such as drug discovery, single-cell modeling, etc.
